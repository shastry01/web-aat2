Social Media Applications

Connecting People Around the World

Facebook

Facebook is a popular social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends and family, share photos, videos, and updates.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video sharing app widely used by young people to share stories, reels, and visual content.

WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a messaging application that enables text messaging, voice calls, video calls, and file sharing.

Twitter (X)

Twitter is a microblogging platform where users post short messages called tweets to share opinions and news.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform used for career growth, job searching, and business connections.
